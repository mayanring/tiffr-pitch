Rushline is a festival planning app for film lovers and festival organizers.

  • So simple, even your intern can handle it.

    Easily manage your film and event data using our streamlined content management system, custom built for festivals.

  • Your films. Lovingly presented.

    Beautiful, mobile-ready film pages are created for you as you add films to your festival.

  • Make a list. Check it twice.

    Film enthusiasts craft a shortlist of their favorites as they browse your films and can easily share them with their friends, building buzz for your festival.

  • Goodbye, spreadsheets. Hello planner.

    Our festival planner makes it easy to identify scheduling conflicts and only shows events for your favorite films, helping you to focus on quickly building a schedule.

  • Calendar integration.

    Schedules can be added to any calendar app for convenient offline access during the festival. We make it easy for your customers to share this online too.

  • Listen to your customers.

    Gather useful data about your customers, your films, and your events, and gain insights for running your festival more efficiently

Who are you guys?

We are Ryan Ming and Mina Mikhail, Toronto-based engineers, designers and film buffs with our fair share of experience working in the film and video-on-demand industries.

We love film and the excitement of the festival, but we were frustrated by the amount of time it would take to create a personal schedule from the hundreds of films showing at the Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF) each year. We built the first version of Rushline (as tiffr.com) to save ourselves some time and grey hairs. Over the last four years, we've amassed a modest but very loyal group of users who love what we're doing: taking what was once a daunting and time-consuming process and making it fun!

Now we want to build out the platform to serve other festivals because we believe that a streamlined festival experience can translate into more sales and fewer empty seats.

One more thing

Industry professionals have different needs from your patrons. We get it. We're cooking up features specifically targetted to journalists, buyers and sellers. We think you'll love it.
