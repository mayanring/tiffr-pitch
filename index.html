Our Story

We love film and the excitement of the festival, but we were frustrated by the amount of time it would take to create an itinerary from the hundreds of films showing at the Toronto International Film Festival each year. So we built the first version of tiffr to solve the problem for ourselves and shared it online with little to no fanfare. Over the last four years, we've amassed a modest but very loyal group of users who love what we're doing: taking what was once a daunting and time-consuming process and making it fun!

Now we want to build out the platform to serve other festivals.

We believe that a streamlined festival experience can translate into more sales and fewer empty seats.

What is Rushline?

Rushline is a festival planning app for film lovers, industry professionals, and festival organizers.

  • Easily manage your film and event data using our streamlined content management system

  • Beautiful, easy-to-use and mobile-ready film pages are created for you as you add films to your festival

  • Film-lovers and industry professionals build shortlists of favourites as they browse

  • The festival planner keeps track of which favorites have been scheduled and allows attendees to create an itinerary quickly and painlessly

  • Attendees can share their shortlists and itineraries, building buzz for your festival

  • Itineraries can be imported into any calendar app for convenient offline access

  • Gather useful data about your customers, your films, and your events, and gain insights for running your festival more efficiently
