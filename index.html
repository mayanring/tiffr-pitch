Rushline is a festival planning app for film lovers, industry professionals, and festival organizers.

  • Easily manage your film and event data using our streamlined content management system
  • Beautiful, easy-to-use and mobile-ready film pages are created for you as you add films to your festival
  • Film-lovers and industry professionals build shortlists of favourites as they browse
  • Our festival planner saves attendees tons of time by focusing solely on screenings for shortlisted films, allowing them to finalize their schedules quickly and painlessly
  • Shortlists and schedules can easily be shared, building buzz for your festival
  • Schedules are accessible to festival-goers from anywhere there's Internet, and also integrate with any calendar app, so they never miss a beat (or a film!)

Originally built as a companion app for use with the Toronto International Film Festival, Rushline simplifies the daunting experience of planning a schedule from hundreds of films and makes it fun. Now we want to build out the platforms to serve other festivals.

What makes tiffr different?

We love film and the excitement of the festival, but we were frustrated by the amount of time it would take to create a schedule from the hundreds of films available at the Toronto International Film Festival each year. So we built tiffr and put it online with little to no fanfare. Over four years, we've amassed a small but very loyal group of users who love what we're doing.

We believe that a streamlined festival experience can translate into more sales and fewer empty seats.

Features

  • Simple Shortlisting

    Your customers browse and shortlist their favorites. You get a snapshot of demand, before you've even announced a schedule. Customers share their shortlists with their friends and build buzz for your festival.

  • Simple Scheduling

    The event planner is a customized view of shortlisted films. Apply filters to remove events you're not interested in. Easily identify conflicts in your schedule.

  • Simple Sharing

    When you're done, view your selected events on your personalized itinerary. Add your schedule to your favorite calendar app.
