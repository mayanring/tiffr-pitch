Rushline is a companion shortlisting and scheduling application originally built for use with the Toronto International Film Festival, the world's largest public film festival. It simplifies the daunting experience of planning a schedule from hundreds of films and makes it fun. Now we want to build out the platforms to serve other festivals.

What's this tiffr thing?

tiffr is the name we use for the TIFF-specific version of Rushline, which has been serving the TIFF-going community for the past 4 years.

Features

  • Simple Shortlisting

    Your customers browse and shortlist their favorites. You get a snapshot of demand, before you've even announced a schedule. Customers share their shortlists with their friends and build buzz for your festival.

  • Simple Scheduling

    The event planner is a customized view of shortlisted films. Apply filters to remove events you're not interested in. Easily identify conflicts in your schedule.

  • Simple Sharing

    When you're done, view your selected events on your personalized itinerary. Add your schedule to your favorite calendar app.
