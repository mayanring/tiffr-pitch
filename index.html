Introducing

Rushline

The simplest way to plan your festival

Rushline is a festival planning app for festival organizers and film lovers.

Made for organizers

  • Focus on your festival

    Easily manage your film and event data using our streamlined content management system, custom built for festivals.

  • Dress to impress

    Beautiful film pages are created for you as you add films to your festival.

  • Save time & frustration

    Our proven workflow takes the daunting task of making a festival schedule from hundreds of films fun and easy.

  • Generate buzz

    Empower your audience to share their festival favourites and schedules with their friends, family and social networks.

  • Know your audience

    Gather useful data about your customers, your films, and your events, and gain insights for running your festival more efficiently.

  • Take it mobile

    Reach your audience on all devices, from smartphones to desktop computers and everything in between.

Made for film lovers

Festival planning is fun, easy, and saves your audience tons of time with our proven workflow.

  • Pick favourites

    Festival-goers build a shortlist of their favourites as they browse through your programming in our magazine layout.

  • Plan it out

    Our festival planner presents events for shortlisted films and makes it easy to identify scheduling conflicts, helping your audience focus on quickly building their personalized schedule.

  • Spread the word

    Now that the hard work is done, sharing with friends, colleagues and social networks is just a click away. Compare shortlists, coordinate schedules and earn bragging rights.

  • Pros are covered, too

    We present both the public and press & industry schedules in the festival planner to cater to your entire audience.

  • Calendar integration

    Schedules can be added to any calendar app for convenient offline access during the festival.

Number crunching

After years of pen and paper planning for the Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF), we created tiffr.com in 2009 to save ourselves time and grey hair. Over the last four years, we've amassed a modest but very loyal group of users who love what we're doing: taking what was once a daunting and time-consuming process and making it fun.
